1. Introduction

SiteWatch application ("App") is a tool that helps users monitor changes on websites. This privacy policy explains what information the app collects, how it uses and protects it.

2. Information Collected

SiteWatch DOES NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Location information
  • Contacts list
  • Photos or files

SiteWatch only uses:

  • URLs you enter: Stores website addresses you want to monitor ON YOUR DEVICE. This information never leaves your device.
  • Internet Access: Uses internet connection to check the content of specified websites.
  • Background Work: Uses WorkManager to check sites at specified intervals.

3. Use of Information

Information collected by the app:

  • Is only used to check the websites you specify
  • Is stored locally on your device (SharedPreferences)
  • Is never sent to any server or third party
  • Is protected in a way that only you can access

4. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: Used for displaying ads. AdMob has its own privacy policy: AdMob Privacy Policy

5. Permissions

The app uses the following permissions:

  • Internet (INTERNET): To check websites
  • Notifications (POST_NOTIFICATIONS): To send you notifications when sites change
  • Background Work (WAKE_LOCK, RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED): For continuous monitoring

6. Data Security

Your data is stored on your device using Android's secure storage mechanism (SharedPreferences). The app does not encrypt or transmit your data over the internet.

7. Children's Privacy

The app is not intended for children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from users under 13.

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
